WE ARE ON THE MAP

Places to visit and be amazed!!!

Peaks for climbing.

In the mountains of the Batken Territory there are peaks that attract climbers from all over the world. These are the peaks of Asan, Odessa and others. Of particular interest to climbers is Piramidalny Peak, rising 5509 meters above sea level, although there are plenty of other objects.

The raging stream of Abshyr-Ata, bursting out of the rocks with a roar!

A cascade of spray here forms a real veil of fog. Yes, such that in a couple of seconds we were wet like mice. A real riot of nature. It is also surprising that this powerful jet of white-foamy water beats straight out of the rock, like a broken tap of the gods. The height of 20 meters above the ground, from where the "leak"!

The Kan Fortress is a real rarity of the Batken region!

On the southern slope of the Zhaman-Chul spur, in the Shadymir tract, there is a tannic cave Kan-i-Gut (locally called Zauk-Kur). In the X-XI centuries, silver and other valuable ores were mined here, as even Avicenna wrote about. The length of the cave is more than 30 km!

One of the popular places for Batken residents is the Tert-Kul reservoir.

There is also a place near the regional center, beloved by the Batken people, where you can cool off under the hot sun. This is the Tert-Kul reservoir, which holds up to 90 million cubic meters of water, and whose bottom was once covered with plastic sheets in order to avoid unnecessary leakage.
