Horse rides

Horse riding is a combination of horseback riding and excursions to natural attractions.The cost of renting a horse for one day in different regions of the country varies from 3 to 10 dollars.

Falcon hunting

Falconry - hunting for wild animals with the help of specially trained golden eagles and falcons. The cost of entertainment depends on the experience of the berkutchi and varies from 100 to 300 dollars

Mountaineering and trekking

Climbing and trekking - climbing hard-to-reach mountain peaks and hiking in the natural environs of the Batken region do not provide for payment. But for setting up a tent, you will have to pay from 5 to 30 dollars.

Rafting

Rafting is rafting on mountain rivers. The cost of a rafting tour on the most popular sections of the countries rivers varies from 50 to 150 dollars.

Cultural tourism

Historical and cultural tourism - visiting and exploring places of historical importance. Ancient stone statues and rock paintings (petroglyphs) are popular in Kyrgyzstan.

Speleotourism

Speleotourism - visiting caves and overcoming obstacles in them with special sports equipment. You can also find unique rock paintings in the Kyrgyz grottoes.
